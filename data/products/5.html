Unique in the industry.

The Company® OMNIBEND® Fiber is composed of proprietary expanded polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) with specific engineered physical qualities. The fiber acts as a durable dry barrier to abrasion, reducing friction and elevated temperatures normally caused by friction. This technology also enhances the performance of custom coatings. The result: The Company® OMNIBEND® Fiber can vastly increase a rope's service life and reliability.
